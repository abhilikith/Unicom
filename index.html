Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about placing orders and procuring through UNICOM SUPPLIES.

Are all products supplied by UNICOM 100% genuine and under warranty?

Yes. Every single item supplied—from laptops to printer cartridges—is sourced directly through authorized OEM channels. All products carry standard official manufacturer warranties.

Do you support bulk B2B and institutional procurement?

Absolutely. We specialize in corporate IT procurement for companies, schools, hospitals, and government bodies, offering tiered bulk pricing, custom billing, and dedicated account managers.

Do you provide nationwide shipping and delivery?

Yes. We partner with leading logistics providers to ensure fast, insured shipment of critical hardware, switches, servers, and accessories right to your facility.

What IT maintenance and repair services do you offer?

We provide on-site hardware troubleshooting, structured network cabling, laptop chip-level repairs, printer fleet servicing, and customized Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC).

Upon submitting your requirement via our contact form or email, our sales team typically returns a formal quotation within 1 business day.
